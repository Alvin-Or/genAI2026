# Group 3: Government App

## Project Title
Government App Analysis

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Hong Kong government mobile applications and digital services, examining their effectiveness, accessibility, and areas for improvement.

## Research Framework

| Category | Focus Area | Questions |
|----------|------------|-----------|
| Usability | User Experience | How user-friendly are government apps? |
| Accessibility | Inclusive Design | Are apps accessible to all citizens? |
| Functionality | Service Delivery | Do apps effectively deliver public services? |
| Adoption | Usage Statistics | How widely are government apps used? |

---

## Key Areas of Investigation

### 1. Government Digital Services
- Overview of available government mobile applications
- Services provided through digital platforms
- Integration across different government departments

### 2. User Experience
- Ease of use and navigation
- Accessibility features for people with disabilities
- Multi-language support

### 3. Service Effectiveness
- Comparison of digital vs. traditional service delivery
- Response times and efficiency
- User satisfaction levels

### 4. Data Privacy & Security
- Personal data protection measures
- Security protocols
- Compliance with privacy regulations
